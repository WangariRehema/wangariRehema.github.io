the names ,Rehema Wangari. A visual story teller using photography as my muse. I have been telling stories for 4 years now with my photography.
Encouraging and shifting mindsets on how to view and value oneself. With no exceptions, I take the time to understand the brief before embarking on any project.
I don’t use a one size fits all approach because all projects are different. I let my subjects be as natural as possible as not to reduce the natural feel of the project.
I am ready to get challenged in a competitive environment to achieve both personal and organizational goals by hard work and determination.
Working in an environment that appreciates innovation, creativity and a “think-outside-the-box” mentality.
I am a self-taught photographer who received a Bachelor’s degree in Business Information Technology from JKUAT in 2016, and
has worked as a professional photographer for 4 years.
Skills gathered from my profession have helped me communicate effectively my clients’ needs in the photography projects.
